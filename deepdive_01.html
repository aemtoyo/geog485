Brief Description of Study Area

The area of study is in Northern New Mexico. Chimayo is located 7 miles east along State Road 76. Chimayo has a population of 3,177. Total area of the town in near 5.5 sq miles there are no lakes in the town. However, 2 miles further east is the Santa Cruz Lake. The lake covers 121 surface acres.

Purpose

The area below Santa Cruz lake is being studied because the locals in the area depend solely on water released from this lake to grow their crops. Acequias are known for providing the paths in which the water is distributed to the inhabitants of Chimayo. The acequias will be highlighted in the map.
